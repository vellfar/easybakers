About Us

Welcome to Easy Bakers and Confectioners Ltd, where the scent of freshly baked goods fills the air and your sweetest dreams are brought to life.

Our History

Our story began in 2001, with a passion for baking. Our founder, Mr. Amin with a pinch of love and a dash of creativity brought those classic recipes to life in our very own bakery.
Today, we're proud to be a part of this vibrant community, serving up freshly baked bread, pastries, cakes, and treats that bring people together. Our bakery is a place where tradition meets innovation, where every bite is a delight for the senses, and where every customer is treated like family.
We believe that baking is an art form, and we're dedicated to crafting every item with love and care. From classic favorites to new creations, our menu is designed to inspire and delight. Whether you're a longtime local or just visiting, we invite you to come taste the love and warmth that goes into every single item we bake.
So, come on in, and let's share a slice of joy together!"

A letter from Bread and Cake Family

We are thrilled to welcome you to our community of Bread and Cake and we are greatful to each and everyone who has supported us in this journey since 2001.
After years of honing our craft and perfecting our recipes, we finally took a leap and opened a website so that we can be able to reach each and everyone around the globe.
Our bakery and shop outlets are more than just a place to grab a bite. They are gathering spots, hub for connection and community and we've been humbled by the out-pouring support from our customers, who have become like family to us.
So we thank you for joining us on this journey, whether you're a long time customer, local or just visiting, we can't wait to welcome you into our family.
